About Us

The graceful and unique, the first choice restaurant was established about more than 5 years ago with one simple aim 'to provide genuine, home style Indian cooking just like Indian mums would make'.

We prepare healthy cuisines with healthy aroma and rustic feelings just like Indian mums but we make sure that the taste of the food is well-balanced and that the presentation is modern. We believe that the food must not only delight the taste but also the eyes.

As a result, the first choice restaurant's task acquire form - to confirm that Indian cuisine can be as delicate and finely fair as any of the world's greatest cuisines.

Over more than 10 years, the first choice restaurant has set a reputation on being a popular Indian restaurant in and around Brampton as we have played host to numerous celebrations. Our idea behind our food is to keep our cooking methods and carefully chosen spices and herbs that come in various delicacies entirely from all regions of India even as using meats, seafood and produce that are locally available and as a result we change our menu regularly.

One needs to visit and just look at the Menu to appreciate the adaptability of customs that bring back one's taste buds and leave a lingering taste in one's mouth. the first choice restaurant has been rated as one of the finest Indian Restaurants in and around Brampton with its unique decoration, high quality dine-in experience and trustworthy and delicious take out Indian food delivery service.
